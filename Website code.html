Jessica Holmes

Jessica Holmes is a member elected Co-Director of Good Things Collective, Morecambe. Good Things Collective are a community arts large membership CIC. She is also an active member of Creative West End, a community business partnership that aims to regenerate Morecambe through collaborative creative projects, markets, festivals & events. Jessica is currently setting up a cooperative CIC 3D animation studio called 'Random Acts Studio' This studio aims to create films that are socially and environmentally engaged. She is keen to begin to develop digital skills in the local community in the future through a plan to teach interns.
